About Us

Aptecon Solutions was founded by architect James Wahome. We specialize in designing homes and buildings that combine beauty, function, and sustainability.

Since its founding, Aptecon Solutions has delivered residential, commercial, and eco-friendly projects across Kenya. Our vision is to blend creativity with sustainability, ensuring every design improves lives.

Our mission is to make architectural design accessible to everyone, while delivering professional and creative solutions.
